Project: JuaKazi Gender Bias Detection & Correction System

1. Dataset Description

The evaluation was conducted on a large-scale Kikuyu dataset consisting of 742 sentences. A total of 488 samples were annotated as containing gender bias, covering both pronoun assumption and occupation-related bias categories.

This section reports bias detection performance on the original Kikuyu text prior to any correction. The detector operates under a precision-first configuration to avoid unintended semantic distortion.

6. Technical Interpretation

At scale, the JuaKazi system demonstrates consistent and reliable bias correction for Kikuyu text. Pronoun-based bias dominates both detection and correction volume, with high pre-correction F1 and complete neutralization.

Occupation-related bias remains under-detected, reflected in low pre-F1 despite perfect correction once identified. This indicates that future improvements should prioritize expanding detection coverage rather than correction logic.
